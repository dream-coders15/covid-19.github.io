About the Virus



Corono

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus.

Vaccines

At this time, there are no specific vaccines or treatments for COVID-19.

Spread

The COVID-19 virus spreads primarily through droplets of saliva or discharge from the nose when an infected person coughs or sneezes

Getting to Know

Don't be Panic

Wash your Hand ever 30 Minutes in a day

Put Tissue or Handkerchief in mouth during sneezing

Wear Mask while came outside from home.

Maintain a 2 meters of distance while talking to other persons.

Don't make Crowd in Public Places.

Avoid travelling one place to another.

Don't touch your eyes,Mouth or face.






SYMPTOMS

Symptoms can include fever, cough and shortness of breath. In more severe cases, infection can cause pneumonia or breathing difficulties. More rarely, the disease can be fatal.These symptoms are similar to the flu (influenza) or the common cold, which are a lot more common than COVID-19. This is why testing is required to confirm if someone has COVID-19. It’s important to remember that key prevention measures are the same
